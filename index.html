OUR STORY

VIGNET ESTABLISHED A MANUFACTURING PLANT IN THE PHILIPPINES WITH BRITISH TECHNOLOGY.

Established in 1998, Vignet initially imported uPVC window profiles from the UK for high-end projects in the Philippines. In 2002, the company set up a local manufacturing plant, becoming the sole profile extrusion company. Today, Vignet is the only uPVC window/door profile manufacturer in the Philippines, achieving widespread success in sales across Southeast Asia.
